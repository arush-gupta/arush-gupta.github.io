## My vision for the podcast

The podcast aims to make specialized research accessible to those without a technical background, empowering young learners to make informed educational choices.

It fosters a conversation between an expert (you) and a curious novice (me), exploring your discoveries, fascinations, and philosophical reflections on science. The goal is to offer a holistic view of doing science—how to communicate it effectively, ask critical questions, and challenge assumptions.

As the host, I emphasize that there are no silly questions, only pathways to deeper understanding, revealing the processes and experiences that shape scientific work. 

The conversational format, guided by [tailored questions](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1c66ap61GYgj-QN36hr5Jb1JOMSZ0FZzOMsz9ka9ocdA/edit?usp=sharing), makes complex topics accessible while highlighting the real-world impact of research.
